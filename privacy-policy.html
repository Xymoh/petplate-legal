Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to PetPlate ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application PetPlate (the "App").

PetPlate helps you discover which foods are safe for your pets and which to avoid. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you share with us.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

PetPlate does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the core features of the App. The following information is stored locally on your device:

  • Your language preference
  • Your favorite animals and foods
  • App settings and preferences

Automatically Collected Information

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version of the App. AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising identifier (for personalized ads)
  • IP address
  • App usage data

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Display relevant advertisements (in the free version)
  • Improve the App's performance and user experience

Advertising

PetPlate displays advertisements through Google AdMob in the free version of the App. AdMob uses advertising identifiers and may use cookies and similar technologies to provide personalized advertisements.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

Users who purchase the premium version will not see any advertisements.

In-App Purchases

PetPlate offers optional in-app purchases to remove advertisements and support development. All payment processing is handled by Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Purchase information is managed through RevenueCat, which helps us verify your subscription status. RevenueCat processes purchase data in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All your app preferences, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device using secure storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties (except as described in the Advertising section above).

Children's Privacy

PetPlate is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - For displaying advertisements
  • RevenueCat - For managing in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Google Play Services / Apple App Store - For payment processing

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information. We encourage you to review their privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review any information stored locally on your device
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the App
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (see Advertising section)
  • Request information about data collected by third-party services

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
